More than 200,000 students went abroad for college study in 2012, and the number will keep rising in the coming future, due to the poor local higher education system. Given the big language, cultural and educational gaps, it is quite a big challenge for the young kids to get into their dream schools. Beyond the competing on basic requirements like GPA, TOEFL and SAT scores, students also participate various activities (such as international summer camp) and/or take additional courses (such as AP), hoping to acquire extra edge when applying colleges. TOEFL/SAT training can take up to $40 per hour and a 3-year AP program can cost as much as $10,000. While charging big fees, most of the educational institutes don't have quality teachers and well-developed materials, thus the services they provided is very poor. They not only just waster their customers money, but often times destroy their once-a-lifetime chance to get in to a good college.

None of the things mentioned above are included in the "standard" Chinese eduction system. This means students have to squeeze their spare time and pay big bucks for them.If an online platform can be setup, similar with Coursera but dedicated to helping Chinese students to get into school abroad. Instead of offering courses given by professors, my platform will invite high school / college students who are willing to help the younger kids in preparing tests (SAT, AP, etc.) or simply learn English. The platform is for serious learners and tutors, i.e. all tutoring service are systematically developed and conducted with clearly defined requirements and goals. Most of the tutoring will come at a price (decided by the tutors). The platform will not only host the service but also help the tutors to prepare and organize their materials, and in turn will charge a percentage fee from the tutors' revenue.

FAQ

How to become a tutor?
Any one will passion and willingness to help out can be a tutor. We are specially looking for high-school teachers, top-tier college students, quality education institutes and free-lance tutors. Simple register with us and we will work together with you to set up your tutoring courses.
How is the tutoring conducted?
Once a tutor's course is ready, it will appear on the web's course list. Browsers can read course information such as goals, syllabus, prerequisite and cost. Tutors can decide whether course to be sectional or rolling, one to many or one to one, free or paid. Enrolled students can view course videos and handouts, submit homework's, receive feedback from tutors, and take exams. After the course finished the students will submit review to the course and tutor, which will contribute to the course's score and tutor's reputation.
